Regular Strike Elementary Studio

Rules and procedure for issuing a Regular Strike in Elementary Studio.

At a Glance

Timer5-minute reset
Marks 1 blue mark — 1 tally on both boards
PrerequisiteA friendly reminder must be given first
Issued forBreaking a Hero's Promise

Rules

  • A regular strike requires a friendly reminder first.
  • Regular strikes cannot be issued without a prior verbal reminder.
  • Strike Champions must warn learners before issuing a regular strike.
  • A verbal reminder must precede every regular strike.
  • If the behavior continues after the reminder, the strike is issued and the 5-minute reset begins.
  • One blue mark is added each time a regular strike is issued.
  • One regular strike equals one tally on both boards.

How It Works

  1. Strike Champion observes learner breaking a Hero's Promise.
  2. Gives a friendly reminder referencing the specific promise.
  3. If the behavior continues, the strike is issued.
  4. The 5-minute reset timer begins. One blue mark is recorded.
Refusal: See Refusal for what happens if a learner refuses to reset.
