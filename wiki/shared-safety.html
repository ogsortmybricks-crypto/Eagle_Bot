Safety Rules All Studios

Non-negotiable safety rules that apply to all studios and all learners.

Safety rules apply to all studios equally. Safety non-negotiables apply to every learner regardless of studio.

Non-Negotiable Safety Rules

These rules are enforced as Guardrail Strikes with no prior warning required:

  • No physical aggression of any kind is permitted — no hitting, pushing, or shoving under any circumstances.
  • Physical violence results in an immediate guardrail strike. Physical altercations result in immediate removal from the studio.
  • Bullying or harassment is a non-negotiable guardrail offense.
  • Threatening language is considered a guardrail behavior.
  • Destruction of property is a guardrail behavior. Property destruction is treated as a guardrail offense for both ES and MS.
  • No learner may endanger the physical safety of another.
  • Any act of physical aggression results in an immediate guardrail strike for all studios.

Computer Rules

  • Computers must be carried with two hands.
  • Computers may only be used on the covered patio outside.
  • Computers must not be left unattended on stairs or soft furniture.
  • Never sandwich objects inside a closing laptop.
  • If a rule violation breaks a computer, a twenty-minute strike is issued.
