Shared Space Rules All Studios

Cubby and Lost and Found rules that apply to all learners.

Cubbies

  • Removing items from another learner's cubby without permission is prohibited.
  • Placing unknown items in another learner's cubby is prohibited.
  • Touching another learner's cubby without permission results in a strike.

Lost and Found

  • No food may be placed in Lost and Found.
  • Verify ownership before placing items in Lost and Found.
