Low Grade Guardrail (LGG) Middle School

The LGG strike category for minor non-negotiable rule breaks in Middle School.

LGG stands for Low Grade Guardrail. It is a separate strike category from regular and guardrail strikes in MS — issued for minor but non-negotiable rule breaks. Low grade guardrails carry specific consequences distinct from regular strikes.

LGG Rules

  • Low Grade Guardrail strikes are given without a reminder for most infractions.
  • The bottle lid is classified as an LGG offense — bottle lid is now a warning first before an LGG is issued.
  • Microwave misuse is considered an LGG offense.
  • LGG is a separate strike category from regular and guardrail strikes in MS.

LGG Triggers

  • Failure to replace a bottle lid (warning first, then LGG).
  • Abandoning the microwave while heating food.
  • Setting the microwave over two minutes.
