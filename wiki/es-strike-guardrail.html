Guardrail Strike Elementary Studio

Rules and triggers for issuing a Guardrail Strike in Elementary Studio.

At a Glance

Timer10-minute reset
Marks 2 red marks — 2 tallies on both boards
PrerequisiteNo reminder needed — immediate

Rules

  • Guardrail strikes are issued immediately without a reminder.
  • Two red marks are recorded for every guardrail strike.
  • A guardrail strike skips the reminder and goes straight to 10 minutes.
  • Guardrail behaviors result in an immediate 10-minute reset.
  • No warning is needed before issuing a guardrail strike.

Guardrail Triggers

  • Physical harm to another learner.
  • Emotional harm or threats.
  • Any safety violation.
  • Exiting the patio without a guide — automatic guardrail strike.
Safety: See Shared Safety for the full list of guardrail behaviors that apply to all studios.
