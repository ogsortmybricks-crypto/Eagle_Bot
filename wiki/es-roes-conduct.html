Classroom Conduct Elementary Studio

Behavioral expectations for ES learners throughout the studio session.

Classroom conduct standards are outlined in the ES Rules of Engagement and apply throughout the entire studio session.

General Studio Conduct

  • Pick up after myself in the studio.
  • Do not distract or annoy other learners.
  • Wait patiently for toys and share them.
  • Offer forgiveness when appropriate.
  • Do not yell in the studio.
  • Place shoes and backpacks against the wall.

Outside Time Rules

  • No swinging sticks around during outside time.
  • No saving swings or chairs — they are available to the first hero to arrive.
  • Bring in whatever you take out (balls, toys, etc.).
  • Cover the small sandbox when done.
  • If an ES learner exits the patio without a guide, they will receive an automatic guardrail strike.

ES Socratic ROE

These rules apply during all Socratic discussions and Town Hall sessions.

  • Your face must be visible (face should show).
  • Only use two cushions/pillows and one chair.
  • Listen with your whole body and keep your head up.
  • Raise your hand to speak; only one person speaks at a time; the speaker calls on the next speaker.
  • Make sure everyone gets a chance to speak.
  • Take a stand (pick one choice) and provide specific evidence.
  • If a fellow learner breaks the ROE, whisper their name and show them a stop hand signal.

Circle Time & DEAR Participation

  • Missing or being removed from the circle after one reminder = 1 "X" on the ROE board.
  • Not reading during DEAR (Drop Everything And Read) after a reminder = 1 "X".
  • Two X's in one week = loss of Fun Friday.
  • X's reset weekly.

Free Time

  • Free time is earned by following studio expectations.
