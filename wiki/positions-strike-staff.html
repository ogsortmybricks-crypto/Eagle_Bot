Strike Staff Positions All Studios

Strike Champion and backup positions for ES and MS.

The strike champion role requires the holder to be eligible. Strike champions may not issue strikes to themselves. Backup strike champions must be ready to assume the role at any time. The highest-ranking strike champion holds apology letters if others are absent.

ES Strike ChampionESVacant
▶

Responsibilities

  • Manages the mark and strike system for Elementary Studio.
  • Tracks blue and red marks throughout each session.
  • Issues friendly reminders before Regular Strikes.
  • Reviews apology letters.
  • Manages reset timers and tracks refusal minutes.

ES has one Strike Champion and three backup positions.

ES Strike Backup #1ESVacant
▶

Responsibilities

  • Steps in when the ES Strike Champion is absent.
ES Strike Backup #2ESVacant
▶

Responsibilities

  • Fills in when both ES Strike Champion and Backup #1 are absent.
ES Strike Backup #3ESVacant
▶

Responsibilities

  • Fills in when all other ES Strike staff are absent.
MS Strike ChampionMSVacant
▶

Responsibilities

  • Oversees the apology letter sign-off process.
  • Must sign off on all apology letters.
  • Approves strike documentation.
  • Issues friendly reminders before Regular Strikes.
  • Tracks all marks during a session.
  • Manages silent lunch timers (30 minutes ending at 12:00).
  • Participates in voting on whether strikes are approved (half the majority required).
MS Strike Backup #1MSVacant
▶

Responsibilities

  • Fills in for the MS Strike Champion when absent.
  • Shares sign-off duties when the champion is unavailable.
  • Signs off on apology letters as part of the three-champion review.
MS Strike Backup #2MSVacant
▶

Responsibilities

  • Fills in when both MS Strike Champion and Backup #1 are absent.
  • Signs off on apology letters as part of the three-champion review.
