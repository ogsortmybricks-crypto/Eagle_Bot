ES Strike System Elementary Studio

How strikes, marks, and consequences work for Elementary Studio learners.

Regular Strike

5-minute reset, 1 blue mark. Friendly reminder required first. Issued for breaking a Hero's Promise.

Guardrail Strike

10-minute reset, 2 red marks. Issued immediately for safety violations — no reminder needed.

Refusal During Reset

1 penalty minute added per minute of refusal. Consequences at 5, 10, and 15 minutes of refusal.
