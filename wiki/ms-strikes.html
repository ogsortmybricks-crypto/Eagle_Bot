MS Strike System Middle School

Strike types, silent lunch, apology letters, LGG, and consequences for Middle School learners.

Strike Rules Overview

  • A strike equals one mark in MS.
  • A guardrail strike equals two standard strikes in MS.
  • Two marks remove the ability to give strikes.
  • Strike counts reset at the beginning of each session.
  • Half the majority must vote to approve a strike.
Position ban: 2 or more strikes results in a 5-week ban from leadership positions.

Silent Lunch

30-minute silent lunch ending at 12:00. Must sit alone in designated area. No talking or interacting.

Apology Letter

Required for guardrail strikes. Handwritten, parent-signed, approved by 3 Strike Champions.

Low Grade Guardrail (LGG)

Separate strike category for minor non-negotiable rule breaks like bottle lid and microwave violations.
