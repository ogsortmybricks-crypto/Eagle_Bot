Kitchen Rules All Studios

Kitchen and lunchroom rules that apply to all learners.

Kitchen rules apply to all studios. Kitchen cleanup is the responsibility of every learner who uses it.

General Kitchen Rules

  • Learners must clean up after themselves in the kitchen.
  • Food must not be left out in the lunchroom.
  • Lunchroom tables must be wiped after each meal.
  • No food is allowed in the studio work areas.
  • Learners may not use others' food without permission.
  • All kitchen items must be returned to their proper place after use.
  • Dishes must be rinsed and placed in the drying rack after meals.
  • Learners who misuse the kitchen lose kitchen privileges.

Microwave Rules

  • Only designated learners may use the microwave.
  • The microwave must be cleaned after each use.
  • Programming the microwave beyond two minutes is prohibited.
  • Learners must not abandon the microwave while heating food.
MS Note: Bottle lid and microwave violations are handled as Low Grade Guardrails (LGG) in Middle School.
