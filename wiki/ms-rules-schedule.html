Schedule & Accountability Partners Middle School

The MS daily schedule and accountability partner system.

MS learners follow a structured daily schedule each session. The daily flow includes morning launch, work time, and closing circle.

Daily Flow

  1. Morning launch — Accountability partner check-ins happen every morning. Morning launch sets the goals for the day and includes squad check-ins.
  2. Core skills block — Independent academic work.
  3. Quest time — Collaborative project work.
  4. Lunch — Free lunch or silent lunch (if on strike timer).
  5. Closing circle — Accountability partners review each other's progress. Learners reflect on the session.

Accountability Partner Requirements

Accountability partners check in at the start of each day. Each accountability partner must complete:

  • Writer's Workshop
  • Quest
  • Journal work
  • Weekly core goals
Shared consequence: If one accountability partner fails to complete any required work, both APs lose Fun Friday.
Exception: If an AP has repeatedly reminded their partner to return to work and been ignored, that learner may bring the issue to the MS Town Hall Leader, who will judge the scenario accordingly.

360° Review ROE

Rules for giving 360° peer feedback at the end of each session.

  • 360° reviews are anonymous.
  • Every review must include at least one piece of Warm feedback and one piece of Cool (constructive) feedback. Two Warm and one Cool is encouraged.
  • Feedback must be as specific as possible while maintaining anonymity.
  • Failure to complete your 360° review will result in losing Fun Friday the next time you earn it.
