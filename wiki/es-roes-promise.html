Hero's Promise Elementary Studio

The daily promise recited by every ES learner at the start of each session.

The Hero's Promise is recited by every ES learner at the start of each day. It is a required part of the ES opening routine and sets the tone for every session. The promise is said together as a group — it is a daily commitment to learning and community. The Hero's Promise reminds learners of their commitment to the tribe.

The Promises

  1. Respect myself at all times.
  2. Respect others in words and actions.
  3. Respect the environment and studio materials.
  4. Work hard even when tasks feel challenging.
  5. Have fun while staying responsible.
  6. Commit to my goals and follow through.
  7. Take my education seriously.
  8. Hold others accountable respectfully.
Strike connection: Breaking a Hero's Promise is the trigger for a Regular Strike. A Strike Champion must give a friendly reminder first before issuing a Regular Strike.
