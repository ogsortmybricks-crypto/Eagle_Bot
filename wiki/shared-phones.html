Phone Rules All Studios

Phone and device rules that apply to all learners in all studios.

Both ES and MS learners must keep phones away during studio time. Phone usage during studio hours is not allowed for any learner.

Phone Rules

  • No phones may be used during studio time.
  • Phones must be stored in bags during all studio sessions.
  • Phones must be silenced and put away for the entire studio session.
  • Phone use is restricted to designated break times.
  • No phones are permitted during town hall sessions. Learners must put phones away before town hall begins.
  • No devices may be used during town hall discussions.
  • Earbuds and headphones follow the same rules as phones during town hall.
  • Using a phone during quests results in a warning.
  • Learners caught using phones during sessions receive a reminder.
