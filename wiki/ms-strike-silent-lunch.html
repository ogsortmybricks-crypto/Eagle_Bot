Silent Lunch Middle School

The silent lunch consequence applied to all MS strikes.

Silent Lunch Rules

  • Silent lunch is a consequence for receiving a strike in MS.
  • If you receive a strike, you must sit alone at lunch.
  • During a strike lunch, you must remain silent for thirty minutes.
  • Strike lunch ends at 12:00.
  • Silent lunch is served in a separate designated area from the rest of the studio.
  • During silent lunch, learners may not interact with other learners.
  • Talking during silent lunch extends the consequence.
  • The silent lunch timer resets each session.
Extension: Talking during silent lunch extends the consequence.
