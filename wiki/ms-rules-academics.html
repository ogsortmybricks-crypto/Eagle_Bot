Academics Middle School

Academic requirements and self-governance expectations for Middle School learners.

MS learners are accountable for their own academic progress each session. Self-governance means MS learners are responsible for their own academic output — quest goals must be set and met each session without guide reminders.

Quest & Core Skills

  • Learners must complete their quest goals before free time.
  • Core skills must be demonstrated each session.
  • Quest projects must be submitted on time.
  • Core skills are reviewed at the end of each session.
  • Quest completion is required before a learner may participate in free activities.
  • MS learners must track their own academic progress.
  • Grade requirements must be maintained to remain in good standing.
  • Failing to meet quest goals affects eligibility.

Core Skills Requirements

  • Learners must complete Lexia PowerUp for reading advancement.
  • Pre-Algebra badges must be completed for math advancement.
  • Algebra Basics must be completed for promotion.
  • Full-time learners must earn two new badges per session.
  • Part-time learners must earn one new badge per session.
  • Learners who fall below grade requirements may receive an LGG.
