Position Eligibility All Studios

Requirements for holding a leadership position at Triumph Academy.

Learners must be eligible to hold any leadership position. Ineligible learners may not run for or hold any position. Eligibility is checked before each election cycle. Becoming ineligible mid-session results in immediate removal from a position.

RuleESMS
Mark thresholdFewer than 10 blue marks
Fewer than 10 red marks		Fewer than 2 marks (black)
Strike threshold—Fewer than 2 strikes (otherwise 5-week ban)
Mark resetPer sessionPer session (marks); time-based (position ban)
ES: ES learners with 10 blue marks or 10 red marks cannot hold a leadership position. Accumulating 10+ marks in a session makes a learner ineligible for the remainder of that session. Leadership eligibility is lost upon reaching mark limits.
MS: A learner who accumulates 2 or more strikes is banned from leadership positions for 5 weeks. A learner with 2 black marks is ineligible for MS leadership roles. Position eligibility resets after 5 weeks following a ban. Strike champions lose their position after becoming ineligible. A learner who is banned from positions cannot serve in any leadership role.
