Refusal During Reset Elementary Studio

What happens when an ES learner refuses to complete their reset timer.

Refusal Rules

  • For every minute a learner refuses to reset, one minute is added to their reset time.
  • Refusal minutes accumulate across the session.
  • Refusal resets cannot exceed twenty minutes total.
  • Refusal to reset is tracked separately from strike marks.
  • Marks reset each session in Elementary Studio.

Refusal Consequences

Refusal MinutesConsequence
5+ minutesProdigy removed for one week
10+ minutesRight to give strikes removed for one week
15+ minutesFun Friday lost
Strike eligibility: A learner who refuses for over 10 minutes cannot issue strikes for the rest of the session.

Probationary Contract (After Return)

When a hero returns after being sent home, they are placed on a 5-day Probationary Contract.

Outcome During ProbationResult
No strikes in 5 daysProbation lifted
1 strikeSent home for the day
3 strikes5-day suspension
After suspension: Return is at the owner's discretion, followed by another 5-day probationary contract.
