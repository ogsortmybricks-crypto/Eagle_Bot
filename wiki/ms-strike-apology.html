Apology Letter Middle School

Requirements for the apology letter process following a guardrail strike in MS.

A guardrail strike requires the learner to write an apology letter. The apology must be completed before privileges are restored. The apology letter must be submitted to the Strike Champion before returning to normal activity.

Apology Letter Requirements

  • MS apology letters must be handwritten.
  • The letter must include proper grammar and a full explanation of the behavior.
  • The letter must include an apology to the school and to the affected person.
  • The letter must include an action plan.
  • A parent must sign the apology letter.
  • The apology must be approved and signed off by three Strike Champions.
  • An apology letter with fewer than three sign-offs is not considered complete.
  • If the apology is insufficient, the learner loses the next Fun Friday.
Absence: The highest-ranking Strike Champion holds letters if others are absent.
