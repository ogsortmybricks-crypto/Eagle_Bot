Town Hall Secretary Admin Tool

Type notes from Town Hall sessions. AI reads the entire constitution and intelligently adds, removes, or edits rules across all relevant pages.

How to use: Type each rule change or decision on its own line (bullets or numbers work too). Click "AI Apply" to have the AI analyze your notes and apply the changes to the correct wiki pages. You can also use "Preview" to see keyword-based categorization first.
