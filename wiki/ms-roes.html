MS Rules of Engagement Middle School

Behavioral expectations for MS learners during studio activities, Socratic discussions, and Town Hall.

MS ROE

These rules apply during all discussions, Socratic seminars, and Town Hall sessions.

  1. Listen carefully with my whole body.
    • Everyone should be able to see your eyes.
    • Looking at the speaker.
    • You can lie down, but your head should be up.
  2. Arrive on time and prepared.
  3. Provide evidence and examples to back up my point.
  4. Only one person speaks at a time.
  5. Raise my hand to speak.
  6. Make sure everyone has a turn to speak before choosing someone again.
  7. Do not block a person from the speaker or screen.
  8. Take Socratic seriously.
  9. No computers during Socratic.
  10. Town Hall is a Socratic, and thus all of the above rules apply.
  11. Participation is mainly an honor system; however, it is encouraged that you participate as much as you are able to.

ROE Mark Consequences

  • First offence: one warning, then you are asked to leave the circle and receive an ROE mark.
  • More than 3 ROE marks in a session: lose Fun Friday (full-time learners). 2 ROE marks for part-time learners.
  • Not coming to any circle time while at school: receive an ROE mark.
  • If you get 3 ROE marks, you lose Fun Friday.
  • If you lose Fun Friday, you must put your computer on the computer desk and sit in the library during Fun Friday.
  • Quest participation counts as 1 ROE mark if not attended.
